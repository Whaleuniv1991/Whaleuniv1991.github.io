About me:
Hi, I’m Amritha— I build high-impact programs and partnerships at the intersection of corporate social responsibility, workforce equity, and strategic communications. With a systems-thinking mindset and deep on-the-ground experience, I help mission-driven teams turn bold ideas into scalable action—through effective program design, executive storytelling, and inclusive collaboration. My work spans continents, communities, and contexts—but is always guided by the same core question: 💡 How do we build systems that truly serve the people they’re meant to? 📍 My Journey in Social Impact: A Timeline of Building with Purpose 🌱 Community-Rooted Beginnings South Asia — 2012–2014 I began my career working alongside first-generation women entrepreneurs in rural South Asia—supporting them to launch small-scale businesses rooted in hand-block printing, turmeric, and natural goods. I helped them make the leap from production to entrepreneurship, navigating new digital marketplaces and carving economic independence in traditionally informal economies. 📚 From Grassroots to the Classroom Southeast Asia — 2015–2017 Eager to work directly with young learners, I stepped into public school classrooms to teach English, math, and entrepreneurship to middle schoolers in under-resourced communities. These were students on the brink of dropping out—and together, we reimagined education as a platform for self-determined futures, not just test scores. 💼 Building Workforce Equity for Marginalized Youth Asia + Global — 2017–2019 I then led skills training and career readiness initiatives for youth with disabilities and early school leavers—designing inclusive programs that helped them access dignified jobs at companies like PepsiCo. These partnerships weren’t just about job placement; they were about shifting how systems viewed talent, potential, and inclusion. 🎮 Scaling Learning Through Technology Silicon Valley — 2019–2021 At edtech startups in Silicon Valley, I translated my classroom insights into product strategy—designing gamified tools that made learning more human, engaging, and scalable. I championed learner voice and equity in every layer of the user experience, making educational technology work better for those most often left behind. 🛠️ Leading Refugee Workforce Innovation Bay Area — 2021–2023 At the International Rescue Committee, I led economic mobility programs for refugee families from Afghanistan, Ukraine, Iran, El Salvador, and beyond. I designed cross-sector partnerships and ecosystem strategies that transformed fragmented services into coordinated, long-term pathways toward self-sufficiency. 🌍 Strategic Program Design for Global Equity Remote / Global — 2023–Present Now at Wevise, I lead strategy and partnerships for workforce equity pilots—building inclusive learning systems, supporting underrepresented technologists, and collaborating with global teams to design programs grounded in impact, insight, and integrity. ✨ Across every chapter, I’ve worked at the intersection of people and possibility—bridging systems and stories, values and action, urgency and equity. 💬 Open to consulting engagements, collaborations, and bold social impact projects where strategy meets purpose.
